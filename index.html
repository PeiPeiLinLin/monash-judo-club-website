About Our Judo

Kanola Judo Club is a Melbourne-based community dojo dedicated to teaching judo to people of all ages and experience levels. Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to refine your skills, our friendly instructors provide a safe and supportive environment to grow your confidence, fitness, and discipline.

The founded on the principles of respect, perseverance, and community. We are welcome everyone to challenge and enjoy the process

Our Programs

We offer structured judo programs tailored to different age groups and experience levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or have prior training, there's a place for you at Kanola Judo Club.

Kids Judo(Ages 5-12): Focuses on discipline, agility, and fun games to develop motor skills and confidence.

Teens & Adults (Ages 13+): Covers technical throws, ground work, and fitness training for both self-defence and competitive goals.

Meet Our Coaches

Our coaching team consists of experienced instructors certified by Judo Australia. Each coach brings a unique background in competition, youth instruction, and self-defence, ensuring all students receive well-rounded guidance.

We pride ourselves on being approachable and encouraging, while maintaining the discipline and respect that define judo. Whether you're a nervous beginner or a returning judoka, our coaches will support your journey every step of the way.
